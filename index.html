What's it all about?

Climate change is mainly the shift in the global weather patterns throughout the world. This can be due to both human and natural factors, however, more recently the impact of humans on the planet have been stressed greatly.

This can only really said to be as a result of human intervention on our planet. The combustion of fossil fuels, increased deforestation and the general increases with livestock cultivation have had major impacts.

What are the Implications?

The issues we are facing include:

  • Losing various species of animals
  • Threatening the future of countries that a close to or below sea level eg. Maldives, The Netherlands, Bangladesh
  • Likely to increase the prevalance of natural distasters like tropical storms and coastal flooding
  • Possibility of crop failures and food sortages

These issues are also becoming more and more problematic as time goes on.

What Actions Could we Possibly Take?

It definitely isn't going to be easy to think of solutions to the major climate issue. However, a major place to start would have to be the changing our source of energy. We need to move away from using fossil fuels and start using renewable energy sources such as solar, wind or hydroelectric.
